1. Introduction

DailyQuote Application ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our DailyQuote mobile application (the "App").

Our App provides daily inspirational quotes, allows you to save favorite quotes, share quotes on social media, and browse different quote categories. You can use our App anonymously without creating an account.

Important: Our App is designed for users of all ages, including children under 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or profile pictures. You can use our App completely anonymously.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain technical information to improve our App's performance and user experience:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: Which quotes you view, time spent in the App, feature usage patterns
  • Technical Data: App crash reports, error logs, performance metrics
  • Approximate Location: General location based on IP address (city/region level, not precise location)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving the App's functionality
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Displaying relevant advertisements (with your consent)
  • Ensuring the App's security and preventing misuse

4. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

4.1 Google Analytics/Firebase Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. Google Analytics collects usage data and analytics information. For more information, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

4.2 Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to monitor app crashes and performance issues. This helps us improve the App's stability. For more information, visit Firebase Privacy Policy.

4.3 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our App. AdMob may collect information for advertising purposes. You can control ad personalization through our App settings. For more information, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

4.4 Social Media Sharing

When you share quotes through social media platforms installed on your device, those platforms may collect information according to their own privacy policies. We do not control or access this information.

5. Advertising and Your Choices

5.1 Ad Personalization

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. You have control over ad personalization:

  • Default Setting: Personalized ads are OFF by default
  • Your Choice: When you first open the App, you'll see a consent dialog where you can choose to enable personalized ads
  • Change Anytime: You can change your ad preferences in the App settings at any time

5.2 Non-Personalized Ads

If you choose not to receive personalized ads, you will still see advertisements, but they will not be based on your interests or behavior.
